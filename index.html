I am a fourth-year PhD student at Chalmers University. I work on the Octopi project.

My work concerns embedded systems and their security. I am currently the lead designer of the SynchronVM project.

Topic-wise, I am broadly interested in the following in no particular order :

  • Compilers
  • Virtual Machines
  • Concurrency
  • Computer Architecture (specifically Microarchitectural Security)
  • Memory Management

I sporadically read about philosophy. I often contemplate the role of time and asynchrony in programming languages, natural languages, music, and the wider physical world. You can find my occasional book reviews on Goodreads.
